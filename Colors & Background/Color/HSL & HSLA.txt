#HSL

-hsl(hue,saturation,lightness)
-Hue is a degree on the color wheel from 0 to 360, 0 is red, 120 is green, & 240 is blue.
-Saturation is a percentege vale, 0% is black, 50% is heither lignt or dark, 100% is white.
Ex:- 
      #color{
             color: hsl(0,100%,50%);
             }

#HSLA

-hsla(hue,saturation,lightness,alpha)
-apha parameter is a number between 0.0(fully transparent) and 1.0(not treanparent at all).
Ex:-  p{
        color: hsla(0,100%,50%,0.5)  
        }
