#RGB

-rgb(red,green,blue)
-color value between 0 and 255
-black:- rgb(0,0,0)
-white:- rgb(255,255,255)
-red:- rgb(255,0,0)
-green:- rgb(0,255,0)
-blue:- rgb(0,0,255)
Ex:-  
     h1{
         color: rgb(214,182,111)
       }

#RGBA

-rgba(red,green,blue,alpha)
-alpha paeameter us a number between 0.0(fully transparent) and 1.0(not transparent at all).
Ex:-
      body{
           color: rgba(255,99,71,0.5)  
           }
