#BOX SHADOW


-For Adding shadow to the box use box-shadow property.
Ex:- 
     div{
        border: 1px solid green;
        width: 500px;
        background-color: yellow;
        margin: 100px;
        padding: 20px;
        box-shadow: 10px 10px 15px 3px grey; /*horizhontwl vertical bluer spred color*/
        }

-Give inside to the box shadow use inset in box-shadow property.
Ex:-
     div{
     box-shadow: 10px 10px 15px 3px grey inset;
     }
